Welcome to EcoSphere Urban Farm & Sanctuary

About EcoSphere Urban Farm & Sanctuary

Located in the heart of Seattle, EcoSphere Urban Farm & Sanctuary is committed to creating a greener and more compassionate world. We utilize innovative farming practices including hydroponics, aquaponics, and traditional farming systems to grow fresh, organic produce. Our farm also serves as a sanctuary for rescued animals, offering ethical care to goats, sheep, chickens, and bees.

Sustainable Farming Practices

EcoSphere operates using environmentally friendly methods, ensuring that every plant we grow and every animal we care for contributes to a healthier planet. We believe in the power of sustainable farming systems to reduce waste, conserve water, and promote biodiversity.

Ethical Animal Care & Organic Produce

Our chickens provide organic eggs, and we raise bees for honey. Every animal is treated with the utmost respect, receiving care that is both compassionate and ethical. Our sanctuary also includes rescued goats and sheep who live in a safe, nurturing environment.
